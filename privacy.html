Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Friendora.io ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, password ,when you register
  • Profile Data: Photos, interests, bio, and other details you provide
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, browser type

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis for improvement
  • To monitor usage of our service
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Union, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Your consent
  • The performance of the contract between you and us
  • Compliance with our legal obligations
  • Our legitimate interests

5. India-Specific Provisions

We comply with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the upcoming Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023:

  • Data is stored on secure servers located in India
  • We implement reasonable security practices as prescribed by Indian law
  • Grievance Officer contact provided as per IT Act requirements

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in these situations:

  • Service Providers: With vendors to operate our service
  • Business Transfers: During mergers or acquisitions
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls to personal data
  • Secure server infrastructure

9. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Content Policy

Users must not post content that:

  • Violates any laws or regulations
  • Contains hate speech or discrimination
  • Is sexually explicit or pornographic
  • Promotes violence or illegal activities
  • Infringes intellectual property rights
  • Contains false or misleading information

12. User Do's and Don'ts

Do:

  • Use real, accurate information in your profile
  • Respect other users' privacy
  • Report suspicious activity
  • Keep your login credentials secure

Don't:

  • Share others' personal information without consent
  • Attempt to access accounts you don't own
  • Use automated tools to scrape data
  • Create fake accounts

13. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside your country where data protection laws may differ.

14. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity and hold certain information.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
