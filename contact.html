Contact Guidelines

Do:

  • Use the appropriate email address for your query
  • Include your username in all communications
  • Be clear and concise in your message
  • Attach screenshots for report submissions
  • Allow specified response times

Don't:

  • Send the same request to multiple addresses
  • Expect instant responses (we're a small team)
  • Include sensitive information like passwords
  • Use abusive or threatening language
  • Send marketing or partnership requests

Note: This is a new platform (launched March 2025) with limited staff. We currently only handle queries via email - no phone support or live chat available. Thank you for your patience as we grow!
