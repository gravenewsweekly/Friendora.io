Our Story

Friendora.io was born from a simple realization: in our increasingly digital world, making authentic friendships has become more challenging than ever. Founded in March 2025 by two visionary college friends, we set out to create a platform that prioritizes real human connections over superficial interactions.

As a fresh project, we're excited to welcome our first members and grow together. Our ambitious goal is to reach 10,000-50,000 active members by 2026, creating a vibrant community where people from all walks of life can find their tribe.
