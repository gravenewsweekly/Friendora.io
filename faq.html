Frequently Asked Questions

Friendora.io is a social platform designed to help users make genuine friendships online. Launched in March 2025, we focus on authentic connections rather than superficial interactions.

We run non-intrusive advertisements to earn revenue and keep the platform free for users. These ads help cover our operational costs. Currently, ads cannot be blocked by browser extensions as they are served directly from our servers.

Currently, usernames cannot be changed after account creation. This is to maintain consistency across the platform. We may introduce username changes in future updates.

Currently, password changes must be requested manually. Please email support@friendora.io from your registered email address to request a password reset. We're working on implementing self-service password changes in a future update.

Account deletion requires manual request. To delete your account and all associated data, please email privacy@friendora.io from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request". This action is irreversible and will remove all your data from our servers within 7 business days.
